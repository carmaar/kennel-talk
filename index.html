About Style Guide UI Mockup Goals

kennel talk.

The newest canine communicaton tool.

Kennel Talk bridges the gap between human and hound. With it's sleek design and straightfoward mechanics, pet owners have the option to really bond with their dogs by gaining a new understanding of their unique idiosyncrasies.

GOAL 1

Provide a streamline way for pet parents to communicate with their furbabies. Animals are fickle. We want to bring owners a quick way to record their pets conversation, allowing them to translate their pet's tantrums at the simple click of a button. That way, owners know exactly what's on their pet's mind when it matters most.

GOAL 2

Use newest AI technology to bring accurate translations to pet owners. Kennel Talk harnesses the power of AI technology to record, analyze, and decipher boofs, woofs and whines-- all in real time. Just press the "record" button and give the application a few seconds to process, and voila! Understanding has never been simpler.

GOAL 2

Encourage organization of previous recordings. All translations have the option of being saved. Once saved, the tape is filed away in the "previous recordings" folder, allowing the user to replay whenever necessary. Kennel Talk encourages familiarity in pet mannerisms. By recording, saving, and listening to specific pets sounds, an owner may be able to recall past sounds and have an idea of what their pet is communicating.
